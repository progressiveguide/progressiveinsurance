Understanding Auto Insurance Coverage Options

Auto insurance coverage determines how financial protection applies after an accident, theft, or unexpected vehicle damage. When requesting Progressive auto insurance quotes, drivers typically choose between liability-only policies or full coverage options based on vehicle value and personal risk tolerance.

Liability Auto Insurance

Liability coverage is designed to pay for injuries or property damage caused to others. It is required in most states and forms the foundation of any auto insurance policy. Liability-only quotes are often lower in cost but do not cover damage to the insured vehicle.
